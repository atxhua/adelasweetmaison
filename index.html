Adela's Sweet Maison

Discover a small-batch pâtisserie bringing together the elegance of French tradition and the comforting sweetness of South American favorites. Specialties include authentic French crêpes, handmade alfajores, pastafrolas, and freshly baked sablé cookies.

Every creation is crafted with care, quality ingredients, and a passion for sharing culture through flavor. Perfect for breakfasts, events, gifts, or simply treating yourself — all pastries are made to order.

About Adela
